Hershey's Chocolate

Sweet. Iconic. Loved Around the World.

Hi, my name is Varman, and today I’m going to talk about chocolate—one of the most loved treats in the world.

People love chocolate for many reasons, especially its sweet taste. Chocolate is made from cocoa beans, which are processed, melted, and mixed with sugar to create the chocolate we all enjoy.

Main Ingredients

  • Cocoa solids
  • Cocoa butter
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Lecithin
  • Flavoring

How Chocolate Is Made

Chocolate starts with cocoa trees. The cocoa pods are harvested, and the beans inside are removed and left in the sun to dry. The beans are then sent to factories, where they are roasted, melted, and mixed with sugar and milk.

Finally, the chocolate is shaped, cooled, packaged, and shipped to stores around the world.

There are many chocolate factories in America and around the world. Most large factories have thousands of workers.

I’ve never been to a chocolate factory, but I’ve watched videos of them, and they look really cool. Some even offer golden tickets for special visits!

Is Chocolate Healthy?

Even though chocolate is tasty, eating too much can be unhealthy. Too much sugar can make you feel tired and sick, so it’s best to enjoy chocolate in moderation.
