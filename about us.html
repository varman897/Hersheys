Moments of Goodness

Hershey’s goal is to bring moments of happiness to people around the world through our iconic chocolate. Our chocolate is made in Hershey, Pennsylvania—home to Hershey’s Chocolate World—where visitors can explore how our treats are crafted.

From classic chocolate bars to our famous Hershey’s Kisses, every product is made with care, quality, and a commitment to joy.

Leadership

Kirk Tanner is the CEO of The Hershey Company, leading the organization with a focus on innovation, responsibility, and growth.

Milton Hershey: A Legacy of Goodness

Milton S. Hershey was more than a chocolatier—he was a visionary, philanthropist, and innovator. His life’s work was driven by compassion, perseverance, and service to others.

He founded schools, community centers, and cultural institutions that continue to thrive today. His belief that business should serve humanity still guides The Hershey Company more than 125 years later.

A Story as Iconic as the Chocolate Bar

1857

Milton S. Hershey is born in Derry Township, Pennsylvania.

1872

Begins his candy-making apprenticeship at age 14.

1886

Founds the Lancaster Caramel Company.

1894

Establishes The Hershey Chocolate Company.

1909

Founds the Hershey Industrial School.

1945

Milton Hershey passes away at age 88, leaving a lasting legacy.
